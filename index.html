Chapter Guide — 챕터마다 무엇을 찾는 곳인가

전체 내용을 공부하는 표가 아니라, 문제 키워드를 보고 어느 챕터를 열지 정하는 용도다.

권1 · 회사 S1-S5

  • S1 코드/AHJ/재난/와일드랜드 지붕/예방점검
  • S2 열전달/연소/HRR/점화/flashover/폭발 이론
  • S3 NFPA 921/화재조사/모델링/hazard-risk/design fire
  • S4 ASET/RSET/피난시간/exit access/egress 설계
  • S5 교육/CRR/5 Es/메시지/juvenile firesetting

권1 · 회사 S6-S11

  • S6 재료성질/독성/분진/NFPA 652/금속/가스/액체
  • S7 저장탱크/화학물질 저장/혼재/폐기물
  • S8 특수장비/laser/oven/ESS/PV/전기설비
  • S9 woodworking/spray/dipping/lab/semiconductor/hot work
  • S10 비상전원/엘리베이터/HVAC/상업주방 hood
  • S11 housekeeping/소화전 배치/site planning/hot work permit

권2 · 집 S12-S17

  • S12 조직/훈련/PPE/dispatch/radio/pre-incident plan
  • S13 현장작전/fire stream/alternate water/hazmat/wildland/ARFF
  • S14 NFPA 72/감지기/통보장치/연동/경보 ITM/CO
  • S15 tank/public water/hydraulics/fire pump/corrosion
  • S16 sprinkler/K-factor/hanger/storage/standpipe/NFPA 25
  • S17 CO2/foam/extinguisher/clean agent/inert gas/explosion prevention

권2 · 집 S18-S21

  • S18 fire barrier/opening/interior finish/smoke/firestop/venting
  • S19 construction type/load/column/beam/truss/내화성능
  • S20 occupancy별 travel distance/health care/assembly/storage
  • S21 tunnel/marine/aviation/rail/service station/EV
